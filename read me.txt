Requires AHK (Auto hot key)


this is a simple script designed for 1920x1080
you can be full screened or windowed

-------
Triggers with Middle mouse (M3)
-------
What the script does:
Moves the mouse to the enemy portrait, right clicks, then left clicks on squelch box, 
then moves the cursor down 0,500 pixels.
-------------

If you want to change the trigger button, edit the autosquelch.ahk file in notepad
look for line 6 Middlemouse::
change "Middlemouse" to whichever keybind you want

link to AHK's keylist: http://www.autohotkey.com/docs/KeyList.htm
